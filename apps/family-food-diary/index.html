Family Food Diary

Track nutrition for the whole family. Monitor calories, protein, vitamins, minerals and other nutrients in one place.

About the App

Family Food Diary helps you keep nutrition records for the entire family. Add meals, monitor nutrient intake and discover eating habits over time.

  • Track nutrition for multiple family members
  • Monitor calories, protein, fat and carbohydrates
  • Analyze vitamins, minerals and micronutrients
  • Discover top foods by iron, magnesium and other nutrients
  • Store your data on Google Drive or Yandex Disk

Your Data

Family Food Diary does not operate its own cloud servers. Your data can be stored in your personal Google Drive or Yandex Disk account, giving you full control over your information.
